OUR SERVICES

HSE INSPECTIONS

At Adbelt Consults Limited, we conduct workplace HSE inspections for their sites, machinery and equipment, processes to identify hazards and risks and develop an action plan of the mitigation measures to address the HSE hazards and risks including machinery hazards, psychological hazards, biological and chemical hazards, ergonomic and physical hazards. This helps our clients to eliminate workplace injuries and cases of ill-health. We follow the international standards in executing our work with unmatched professionalism.

TRAININGS

In our ambition empower to organisations to integrate the best HSE practices with in their operations, we conduct well developed trainings that are tailored to be effective and elaborate to the different sectors. These trainings aid in raising awareness in regards to health, safety and environment. At Adbelt Consults limited, we have qualified and experienced HSE professionals that conduct both practical and theoretical trainings. Our trainings can be on-line or physical depending on the preference of the client. Examples of the trainings we conduct include working in confined spaces, hazard identification and risk assessment, working-at-height, fire safety, lifting safety, electrical safety, control of substances hazardous to health, Behaviour safety etc.
